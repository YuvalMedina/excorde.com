Support Ex Corde’s Production of Prince Cnut

Prince Cnut is a two-act chamber opera by composer Marco Catella and librettist Juan Batalla—a historical drama with mythic weight, brought to life through music that conveys that weight. Produced in collaboration with the NYC Orchestra Project, it requires 13 singers, 15 instrumentalists, a choir, a team of directors, designers, and technicians, a week-long theater rental, multiple rehearsal spaces, costume and set design, and professional recording and filming.
