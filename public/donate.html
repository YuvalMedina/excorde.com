Support Ex Corde

Your donation helps us bring timeless music and bold new voices to life. As a composer-led nonprofit, every contribution directly supports our concerts, commissions, and community outreach.

Your Gift in Action

  • $25 helps cover a musician’s rehearsal session — keeping the music alive behind the scenes.
  • $100 helps bring one chamber piece to life on our concert stage.
  • $250 supports the creation of an original composition by one of our featured composers.

Ex Corde is a registered nonprofit. All donations are tax-deductible as allowed by law.
