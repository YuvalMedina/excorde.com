Yuval Medina

Yuval Medina is a composer and pianist who believes in the power of music to change our world. A storyteller at heart, his music seeks to express all that cannot be said in words. He is currently pursuing his master’s in classical composition at the Manhattan School of Music.

His music was recently featured by Vanity Fair during the Oscars ceremony, accompanying stars including Adrien Brody, Olivia Wilde, and Andrew Garfield. Yuval received the Tribeca New Music Merit Award for his piano cadenza Focusing on Other Things, and was recently awarded an Honorable Mention from the New York Youth Symphony’s First Music competition.

You can follow @yuvalcomposer on Instagram, YouTube, or SoundCloud to stay up to date with his latest work.
