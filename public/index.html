About

Ex Corde is a composer-led ensemble dedicated to curating concerts that speak across centuries. From Bach, Britten, and Mahler to emerging composers from New York City and beyond, our programs invite dialogue between the timeless and the newly imagined. Founded by composers Marco Catella, Joshua DeLozier, and Yuval Medina, we bring our own music into conversation with the masterworks that shaped us — crafting narratives where the past doesn’t just echo, it listens and responds.

Composer Bios

Marco Catella

Marco Catella is a composer and sound artist whose work explores perception, memory, and the intersections between music, language, and space. His music has been performed across the United States and Europe, and he holds degrees in composition from Columbia University and the Manhattan School of Music.

Joshua DeLozier

Joshua DeLozier is a composer and interdisciplinary artist whose work spans contemporary classical music, electronics, and performance art. With a focus on texture, gesture, and storytelling, his pieces often explore identity, embodiment, and ritual. He is currently based in New York City.

Yuval Medina

Yuval Medina is a composer and pianist who creates emotionally charged works that fuse tradition with personal expression. A believer in music’s power to heal and connect, Yuval’s works often bring together different disciplines and cultural narratives. He is currently pursuing his master's in composition at the Manhattan School of Music.
